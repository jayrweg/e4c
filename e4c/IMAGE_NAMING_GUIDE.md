# Image Naming Guide for Empower for Change Website

## Standardized Image Naming Convention

### Team Images (in `/public/team/`)
- `judith-justine-kweka.jpg` - Judith Justine Kweka (Board Chairperson)
- `alice-henry-mbowe.jpg` - Alice Henry Mbowe (Board Member)  
- `mwiru-siima.jpg` - Mwiru Siima (Board Member)
- `team-4-fatuma.jpg` - Fatuma Ali (Disability Inclusion Specialist)
- `team-5-mary.jpg` - Mary Ndege (Health Education Officer)
- `team-6-zainab.jpg` - Zainab Mwalimu (Advocacy Coordinator)

### Project Images (in `/public/projects/`)
- `project-education.jpg` - Reproductive Health Education
- `project-inclusion.jpg` - Disability Inclusion Initiative
- `project-advocacy.jpg` - Community Advocacy

### Gallery Images (in `/public/gallery/`)
- `gallery-1.jpg` through `gallery-6.jpg` - Various gallery images

### About Images (in `/public/about/`)
- `about-preview.jpeg` - About section preview image

### Slide Images (in `/public/slides/`)
- `slide-1.jpg` - Hero slide 1
- `slide-2.jpg` - Hero slide 2  
- `slide-3.jpg` - Hero slide 3

## How to Replace Images

1. **Team Images**: Replace the files in `/public/team/` with your actual team photos, keeping the exact filenames listed above.

2. **Project Images**: Add your project photos to `/public/projects/` with the standardized names.

3. **Gallery Images**: Add your gallery photos to `/public/gallery/` as `gallery-1.jpg`, `gallery-2.jpg`, etc.

4. **About Preview**: Replace `/public/about/about-preview.jpeg` with your about section image.

5. **Hero Slides**: Replace the files in `/public/slides/` with your hero banner images.

## Important Notes

- All image names use lowercase letters and hyphens (kebab-case)
- No spaces in filenames
- Use .jpg for photos, .jpeg for specific cases
- Keep the exact filenames as listed above to maintain website functionality
- Images should be optimized for web (compressed, appropriate dimensions)
